## Implementation Priority

### Quick Wins (Simple to implement, high impact)
- Hover Glow
- Selection Pulse
- Ripple/Shockwave
- Animated Contour Lines
- Circular Gauges

### Medium Complexity
- Procedural Fire
- Rain with ripples
- Dynamic Heatmap
- Flow Curves
- Glitch Art

### Advanced (Complex but impressive)
- Realistic Ocean
- Volumetric Fog
- Aurora Borealis
- Epidemic Spread
- Urban Growth Timeline
